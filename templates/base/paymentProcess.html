Payment Process

We offers multiple payment process method for your order:
  • Credit Cards & Debit Cards(All Schedule Bank)
  • bKash
  • COD – Cash on Delivery ( Only applicable for Dhaka city)
  • We Accept SSL COMMERZ All Payment Method

For COD Payment Process if you don’t want to receive the product after getting the product(Must be intact/Undamaged), you have to pay the courier charge 60Tk and for outside Dhaka City, you need to pay advance with Credit Card

For bKash payment process follow the following procedure:

Our bKash no (01912404971) is a Merchant Account, not a Personal Account. You can pay us only through PAYMENT option, CASH IN (available only in Agent Account) or SEND MONEY option will not work for us.

You need to have your own bKash account to pay us. Registering bKash is free and it takes only few minutes. However, if you don’t a have a bKash account, you can pay through someone who has bKash. You can even ask your nearest bKash agent to pay us through their Personal Account.

  • Go to your bKash Mobile Menu by dialling *247#
  • Choose “Payment”
  • Enter the bKash Wallet Number 01912404971
  • Write the amount you want to pay
  • Enter a reference* against your payment (you can mention the purpose of the transaction in one word. e.g. Bill)
  • Enter the Counter Number “1”
  • Now enter your bKash Mobile Menu PIN to confirm

Done! You will receive a confirmation message from bKash.
